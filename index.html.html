How the numbers work. The favourite is the team with the shortest pre-match three-way moneyline. Odds shown are the published pre-match price for each fixture (FOX Sports / sportsbook consensus), converted to decimal. A win pays £100 × (decimal − 1); a draw or upset is −£100. Exact prices at any single bookmaker (e.g. Bet365) will vary by a few points, but the favourite and the approximate return are the same.

Results & odds: FourFourTwo, FOX Sports, ESPN, CBS Sports, Oddschecker (June 2026). Tournament runs 11 June – 19 July 2026; this ledger covers every completed match and is built to be topped up after each matchday. Not betting advice — a record of a hypothetical flat-stake strategy.
